CRON-o-Meter Web Version

The same great easy-to-use tracking of CRON-O-Meter is now available as a web application at http://cronometer.com/.

Why switch to cronometer.com?

  • Access your food diary from any web browser
  • Track on-the-go with the iPhone or Android app
  • Easily share your foods, recipes, and progress with others
  • A larger and growing food database
  • More detailed charts and graphs
  • Track calories burned in exercises
Once you have created an account at cronometer.com, you can upload your data to your online account under the Help Menu -> Export to cronometer.com...
